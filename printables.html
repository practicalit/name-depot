Bring your own idea

One effective way to promote people's names is by printing on wearable items such as t-shirts, hats, sport wears and jackets. These items not only serve as everyday apparel but also act as mobile advertisements. They can be tailored to different demographics, from children's sports teams to corporate employees, thus maximizing reach and engagement.

Another innovative idea is to print on practical everyday items that people use frequently, such as water bottles, tote bags, pens, and phone cases. These items are functional and have a high utility value, ensuring that the printed names receive repeated visibility. For example, branded water bottles are perfect for gyms, offices, and outdoor activities for advertising a name. Phone cases, being constantly in view, offer a prime spot for personalization and branding. These items not only enhance brand recognition but also serve as thoughtful, useful gifts that people appreciate and use regularly.
